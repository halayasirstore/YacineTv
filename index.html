What we collect

Yacine TV does not require sign-up, login, or any personal account. We only handle the minimum data needed to deliver scores, standings, news, and notifications.

Information collected automatically

  • Anonymous device ID — a random identifier generated on first launch, used so your favorites and notification subscriptions persist across sessions.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) push token — required to deliver real-time notifications about kickoff, goals, half-time, and full-time.
  • Device platform (Android / iOS) and language code (en / ar / fr) — so notifications are sent in the right language.
  • App version — included in crash logs to diagnose problems.
  • IP address — kept in standard server logs for abuse prevention only (max 30 days).

Information you choose to provide

  • Match favorites — when you tap the star ⭐ on a match.
  • Per-match notification preferences — when you tap the bell 🔔 on a match.
  • Language preference — saved locally for the next launch.

What we don't collect

The following are never requested or accessed:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or any account information.
  • Your physical location — no GPS, no ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION, no ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION.
  • Your contacts, photos, microphone, camera, calendar, or files.
  • Any biometric data.
  • Browsing history outside our app.

Android permissions we request

Every permission below is essential for a single, specific feature. We request nothing else.

INTERNET

Fetch live scores, standings, news, and team data from our servers.

POST_NOTIFICATIONS

Required on Android 13+ to display match-event notifications.

WAKE_LOCK

Wake the device briefly when an important notification arrives.

RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED

Restore the FCM connection after device restart so notifications keep arriving.

VIBRATE

Vibrate when a notification arrives, if your channel settings allow it.

How we use your data

We process your data only to:

  • Display live scores, standings, news, lineups, and statistics.
  • Send push notifications for matches you have subscribed to via the bell 🔔.
  • Remember your language, favorites, and preferences across sessions.
  • Show advertising (see §5 below).
  • Diagnose crashes and improve performance.
We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to anyone — ever.

Personalized advertising

Where required by law (GDPR, CCPA, etc.), the App displays a consent dialog before any personalized ad is shown. You can withdraw consent at any time from your device settings:

  • Android 12+: Settings → Privacy → Ads → "Delete advertising ID"
  • Android 11 and below: Settings → Google → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalization"
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

Data storage and retention

On your device

A local SQLite database and SharedPreferences store cached match data, language settings, favorites, dismissed notifications, and your FCM token. You can wipe everything by uninstalling the App or by clearing app data in Android settings.

On our server

We keep your device_id, fcm_token, language code, platform, and your match-subscription rows. These are removed when:

  • You uninstall the App and your push token becomes invalid (we automatically delete dead tokens).
  • You request deletion via email (see §13).

Server access logs

Standard web-server logs containing IP and request lines are kept for a maximum of 30 days for abuse prevention, then rotated.

How we protect your data

  • All API traffic is encrypted in transit with HTTPS / TLS.
  • Push notifications are delivered through OAuth-signed FCM v1 API.
  • We do not store passwords, payment information, or any personally identifiable data beyond the anonymous device_id.

No method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We use commercially reasonable measures but cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us at jwatchtech@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (GDPR, CCPA, UK-GDPR, etc.) you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about your device.
  • Delete your device_id and notification subscriptions from our server.
  • Withdraw consent for personalized advertising.
  • Object to processing for direct marketing.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority.

To exercise these rights, email jwatchtech@gmail.com from the device's email account or include the install date and device model so we can locate the relevant subscriptions.

International data transfers

Our servers are hosted by Hostinger and Google Firebase in data centers that may be located outside your country. By using the App you consent to the transfer of your data to these locations under standard contractual safeguards.
